# Agile (Scrum) Method Template

Group tasks into sprint-sized user stories with acceptance criteria.

## Story Format

```markdown
### Story S{NN}: {User story title}
**Points:** {1|2|3|5|8}
**Sprint:** {N}
**Acceptance criteria:**
- [ ] {Criterion 1}
- [ ] {Criterion 2}

**Tasks:**
- T{XX}: {task}
- T{YY}: {task}
```

## Sprint Plan

| Sprint | Stories | Total Points |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| Sprint 1 | S01, S02 | 8 |
| Sprint 2 | S03, S04 | 10 |
| Sprint 3 | S05 (rollout) | 5 |

## DAG Additions

- Stories are groups of tasks
- Sprint boundaries are milestones
- Demo/review checkpoint after each sprint
